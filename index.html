Co2 Tracker and information

This website displays a widget of the recorded amount of carbon dioxide in the air recorded in september and will automatically update every september when new readings are updated. The gif displays the Co2 hotspot data from 2006 to 2007, not including methane, to give a general understanding of where co2 is being released the most each month of the year. Finally, the graph displays hourly and daily averages everyday for the past month on the amount of co2 in the atmosphere and will update along with the NOAA's readings.
